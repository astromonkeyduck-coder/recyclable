Recyclables

Items accepted in your recycling cart include:

Plastic

Bottles and containers. Empty and clean, caps stay on. 

Glass

Bottles and jars. Empty and clean, remove lids. 

Metal

Aluminum, steel and tin cans. Empty and clean.

Cardboard

Flattened boxes

Paper

Newspapers, paper bags, junk mail, sheets of paper and drink cartons.

Do not include:

  • Food waste
  • Plastic bags
  • Polystyrene foam cups and containers
  • Aluminum food pans

Plastic bags harm recycling machinery, empty the recycling directly into the cart.
