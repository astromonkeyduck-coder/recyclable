# CED Visual Coverage Matrix — All Topics

This matrix maps every topic in the APES Visual Exam Atlas to the CED learning objectives and tracks visual coverage across all content categories. All ~103 topic cards are listed (including supplemental sub-topics 1.11b, 1.11c, 6.5b, 6.6b, and 6.14).

**Legend:**
- ✅ = Present and complete
- ⚠️ = Present but could be enriched with transcript data
- ❌ = Missing or not applicable
- Confidence: H = High (CED-verified), M = Medium (inferred from CED), L = Low (supplemental/extrapolated)
